About Us

JAMS & Associates is a full time Company Secretary firm with offices in Jaipur, Alwar and Bhiwadi having alliances with associate company secretaries, chartered accountants, lawyers in more than 7 States. This network of alliances gives the benefit to the clients of a single window service provider, to deal with all kind of matters across the country under one umbrella.

Under the able direction of 5 partners and 20 personnel, Jams’ team provide you cost effective, quality opinions, innovative and practical solutions for your businesses. Our interdisciplinary approach renders to give you seamless value.

Our services are aimed at protecting our client’s interests by adopting transparent processes and adhering to highest ethical standards, we ensure client confidentiality and our own credibility. The firm’s expertise coupled with effective, timely and practical solutions are the factors which are predominant in choosing the firm by the clients.
